汉字中的中国文化
La Culture Chinoise à travers les Caractères

Starting from oracle bone inscriptions, this trilingual course traces how Chinese characters evolved from concrete scenes to abstract symbols — and how they encode Chinese civilization. Each of 12 lessons opens with 2–3 oracle bone characters, and closes with a Sino-French Cultural Mirror for French-speaking students.
